Overview

A clean, lightweight resource pack that refreshes GUI menus, enhances block models (especially doors and trapdoors), and adds subtle vegetation animations using internal shaders—all while keeping the vanilla Minecraft feel.

Doors and trapdoors have been redesigned with custom 3D models and depth for all wood types.

Menu refresh—updated textures for Crafting Tables, Furnaces, Anvils, and more.
